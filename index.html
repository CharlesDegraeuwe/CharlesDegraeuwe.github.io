OUR WORK
DE DIEFTE De Diefte is a Belgian detective-thriller directed by Raf De Graeve, exploring the infamous 1934 theft of the Ghent Altarpiece panels. We contributed as cameraman and editor, capturing and assembling footage for this community-driven film. The project is set to premiere in December 2025 at CC Nova in Wetteren, highlighting local talent and historical storytelling. VIEW MORE
